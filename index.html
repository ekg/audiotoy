Audio Toys

Interactive sonic experiments for your browser

Constellation Engine
Place stars in a dark sky. A sweep beam plays each one as it passes — brighter stars sing louder, higher stars sing higher. Watch your constellation slowly drift and evolve.
Dream Typewriter
Type freely and every letter becomes sound. Vowels sustain, consonants crack, spaces rest. Words become melodic phrases shaped by their phonetic soul.
Pendulum Choir
Drag pendulums to set their length, then release. Shorter swings sing higher. As they phase in and out of sync, shimmering chords emerge and dissolve.
Volcano Drum
Tap a dark, seething surface to erupt procedural percussion. Position shapes timbre, speed builds polyrhythm, and ghost echoes play your patterns back.
